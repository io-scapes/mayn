Are codescapés internships paid?

Codescapés offers a free internship program where participants do not receive financial compensation for their involvement. While the program provides valuable hands-on experience, it does not offer payment to interns. This approach aims to provide learning opportunities without the financial burden typically associated with internships. Participants are required to pay a documentation fee for certification purposes, but the internship itself is unpaid.

Do I need a technical background to apply?

No, a technical background is not required for the Codescapés Internship. The program is open to applicants from diverse backgrounds, providing opportunities for individuals without prior technical experience to explore the convergence of code, art, and the natural world. This approach enables a wider range of participants to gain hands-on experience and develop multidisciplinary skills in a supportive environment.
